About Mandalika

Mandalika is a tourist area of ​​20,035 hectares located in Central Lombok Regency, West Nusa Tenggara. Since 2017, Mandalika has been inaugurated as a tourism Special Economic Zone (SEZ) which is planned to become a tourist area. The name Mandalika comes from the name of a princess in folklore related to the Nyale celebration in Lombok.
